# STAGE-007-020: Integrations → Unifi panel (device table + drill-down + threats/DPI/Teleport/controller)

## Status: Not Started

## Overview

The **Integrations → Unifi** panel — Unifi-as-a-product. Header status strip (controller up/down · device
up/total · active-threat indicator) + a flat **device table** (8 devices) with click-through **device
drill-down** (switch→ports/PoE; AP→radios/airtime/satisfaction; PDU→outlet relays; UDM→system), plus
**threats/IDS-IPS**, **DPI top-apps**, **Teleport/VPN**, and **controller/API health** widgets. Flat table,
NO topology graph (deferred enhancement).

**Vertical slice:** navigating to Integrations → Unifi renders the panel with a live header + device table;
clicking a device opens its drill-down; threats/DPI/Teleport/controller widgets render live data.

**Spec references:** spec §9.2 (per-integration panel). EPIC-007 "UI structure" (surface 1) + "Scope-outs"
(table only, no topology graph). EPIC-005 STAGE-005-022 (panel shell precedent). STAGE-007-019 (the
endpoints).

(switch ports/PoE, AP radios/airtime/satisfaction, PDU outlet relays, UDM system).

Threats/IDS-IPS list; DPI top-apps; Teleport/VPN state; controller/API-health.
