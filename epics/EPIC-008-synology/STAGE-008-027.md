## Overview

Fill the remaining Synology page widgets (consuming the 008-024 detail endpoints): the backup/replication
timeline (per-job last-run/result/age + the `NoBackupConfigured` state), updates & packages (DSM update +
per-package update/status), Security Advisor status + per-category findings, active-connections (the live

security, connections, and mount-health.
