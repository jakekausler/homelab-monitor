# STAGE-008-017: Surveillance license & HomeMode collector

## Status: Not Started

## Overview

Collect the remaining Surveillance Station status signals: license usage (`SYNO.SurveillanceStation.License
Load` — recon: 3/3 used, max 90) and HomeMode armed/disarmed state (`SYNO.SurveillanceStation.HomeMode
GetInfo` — `on=false` = away/armed). License-exhausted means a camera can't be added or has lost its
license; HomeMode is the surveillance arming posture.

**Vertical slice:** after this stage `homelab_synology_ss_license_*` (used/total/max) +
`homelab_synology_ss_homemode` (armed/disarmed) + schedule flags are emitted for the live SS at 5m.

**Spec references:** §6.2. EPIC-008 "Verified deployment reality" (License.Load; HomeMode.GetInfo).

## Concrete deliverables

### 1. License & HomeMode collector

`ss_license_homemode.py`: calls `client.ss_license()` + `client.ss_homemode()`, emits:
- `homelab_synology_ss_license_used`, `_total`, `_max`, `_local_cam_count`.
- `homelab_synology_ss_homemode` (1=on / 0=off → armed/disarmed semantics),
  `homelab_synology_ss_homemode_schedule_enabled`, notify/streaming/rec-schedule flags.

### 2. Tests

3a fixtures: License (3/3, and a used==max exhausted case); HomeMode on + off. 3b live emission.
