Skips malformed notifications (non-dict, missing/empty notification_id).

- `apps/monitor/homelab_monitor/kernel/api/routers/integrations_home_assistant.py` — added `GET /api/integrations/home-assistant/notifications` endpoint (`Depends(require_session())` + `Depends(get_ha_ws_client)`).
